Cheese-stuffed beef patties with rice

Preparation

Cooking
40'
Waiting

Prepare the juiciest beef patties stuffed with cheddar and cream cheese! Serve them with basmati rice and vegetables for the perfect main course!

Directions

Let's get cooking...
Wash and thoroughly clean the vegetables and herbs.
For the beef patties
Preheat the oven to 180Â°C (350Â°F) set to fan. 
Line a baking pan with parchment paper and set it aside. 
Put the sandwich bread, parsley, mint, 12 g salt, and pepper into a chopper blender, beat until the bread turns into crumbs, and transfer the mixture to a large bowl. 
Add the ground beef, ketchup, and egg. 
Grate the onion in another bowl using the large holes of a box grater, squeeze it with your hands to remove all the moisture, and add it to the bowl with the ground beef. 
Mix all the ingredients with your hands until completely combined. 
Put the cheddar and cream cheese into another bowl, mix them with a spoon until combined, and set the bowl aside. 
Line a small bowl with plastic wrap, place 1/6 of the ground beef inside, and press with your hands to form a well for the filling.
Add 1/6 of the filling into the well, and use the plastic wrap to shape and wrap the meat around the filling, sealing it inside.
Remove the patty from the plastic wrap, shape it as desired with your hands, and set aside.
Place the plastic wrap back in the bowl and follow the same process for the remaining 5 patties.
Place a non-stick frying pan over high heat, add 1-1 Â½ tablespoons olive oil, and let it heat up well. 
Add 3 patties to the pan and cook them for 1 minute on each side.  
Transfer the patties to the lined baking pan, and follow the same process for the rest of them, adding olive oil to the pan again. 
Remove the frying pan from the heat, put the baking pan in the oven, and bake the beef patties for 15-20 minutes, depending on how thick they are. 
Remove the pan from the oven and set aside. 
For the rice
Finely chop the pepper and carrot. 
Place a pot over medium heat and add the rice, water, corn, pepper, carrot, olive oil, salt, and pepper. 
Bring to a boil and then lower the heat. 
Put the lid on the pot and cook the rice for 10-12 minutes until all the water is absorbed. 
Remove the pot from the heat and divide the rice among plates. 
Place 1 beef patty over each plate, and serve.
